## Tianqiao Chen / 陈天桥

Tianqiao Chen is the founder of Shanda Interactive Entertainment, once China's largest online gaming company. He has spent the past decade funding brain science research and, in recent years, building AI directly.

### English

> I spent a decade studying the brain, and in recent years I have been personally building AI. I keep returning to one question: when another form of intelligence arrives in this world, what should we tell our children? Xun Jiang has written his answer in a long letter to his daughter. He does not shy away from anxiety, but neither does he surrender. This is a timely family letter on the eve of the storm.
>
> -- Tianqiao Chen

### Chinese / 中文

> 我花了十年研究大脑，最近几年在亲自做AI，总在想一个问题：当另一种智能来到这个世界，我们该告诉自己的孩子什么？姜迅用一封写给女儿的长信写下了他的回答。他没有回避焦虑，但也没有投降。这是暴风前夜一封及时的家书。
>
> -- 陈天桥

## Back Cover / 封底

An AI-generated line that appears on the back cover of the book, attributed to AI:

### Chinese / 中文

> 一千个人有一千个相同的AI，一千个人能写出一千本不同的哈姆雷特。

### English

> A thousand people have access to the same AI, yet a thousand people can write a thousand different Hamlets.

*Attributed to AI on the book's back cover -- a riff on the Chinese proverb "A thousand readers yield a thousand Hamlets" (一千个读者有一千个哈姆雷特). The line encapsulates the book's thesis: AI commoditizes the average; what remains unique is the person wielding it.*
