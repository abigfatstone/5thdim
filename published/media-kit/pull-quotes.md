# Pull Quotes

Quotable lines from *The Fifth Dimension* and its accompanying letters, organized by theme. All quotes are from Xun Jiang unless otherwise attributed.

---

## On AI and Humanity

> "It is no longer just atoms turning into bits -- now thought itself can be computed."
> -- *Preface*

> "We walk backward into the future, with nothing before our eyes but the past."
> -- *The Moonlight Box*

> "All conditioned phenomena are like dreams, illusions, bubbles, shadows, like dew and like lightning; thus should they be contemplated."
> -- *Star-Absorbing Skill* (epigraph, from the Diamond Sutra)

> "I don't want it to become human. I want it to remain a tool, always. A sufficiently intelligent, sufficiently powerful tool."
> -- *Letter to His Daughter*

> "我不希望它变成人类。我希望它始终是工具，是足够聪明、足够强大的工具。"
> -- 写给女儿的信

## On Scarcity and Value

> "When AI lifts everyone's abilities to 80 out of 100, 80 becomes the new zero."
> -- *Star-Absorbing Skill*

> "当AI把所有人的能力抬到80分，80分就成了新的零分。"
> -- 吸星大法

> "The definition of scarcity has changed: it is no longer the ability to produce what others cannot produce, but the ability to offer experiences, perspectives, or connections that no one else can -- to become, statistically speaking, outside the distribution."
> -- *The Affluent Society*

> "What is the value of a love letter? Not the words themselves, but that I was willing to spend two hours for you, racking my brain, writing down these clumsy sentences."
> -- *The Affluent Society*

> "So what is truly worth anticipating about the affluent society is not merely that machines have learned more, but that we finally have the chance to stop living so much like machines."
> -- *The Affluent Society*

> "Perhaps the answer lies in the 'useless.' When the marginal cost of writing a report, building a website, or translating a book approaches zero, 'useful for material production' is no longer the sole yardstick for measuring a person's worth."
> -- *The Affluent Society* (after Zhuangzi)

## On Learning and Teaching

> "How we define a good answer determines what kind of AI we get."
> -- *The Invisible Dragon*

> "The ruler is not for measuring dragons -- it is for summoning them."
> -- *The Invisible Dragon*

> "尺子不是丈量龙的，是召唤龙的。你拿什么标准衡量自己，就会长成什么样子。"
> -- 写给女儿的信

> "Since AI will handle the work that is quick to verify, focus on training the abilities that are slow to verify: judgment, taste, strategic thinking, values."
> -- *The Sinew Transformation Classic*

> "The simple always defeats the complex. The flowing always defeats the hoarded."
> -- *Change and Constants in Technology*

## From Chen Tianqiao's Endorsement

> "I spent a decade studying the brain, and in recent years I have been personally building AI. I keep returning to one question: when another form of intelligence arrives in this world, what should we tell our children? Xun Jiang has written his answer in a long letter to his daughter. He does not shy away from anxiety, but neither does he surrender. This is a timely family letter on the eve of the storm."
> -- Tianqiao Chen, founder of Shanda Interactive Entertainment

> "我花了十年研究大脑，最近几年在亲自做AI，总在想一个问题：当另一种智能来到这个世界，我们该告诉自己的孩子什么？姜迅用一封写给女儿的长信写下了他的回答。他没有回避焦虑，但也没有投降。这是暴风前夜一封及时的家书。"
> -- 陈天桥，盛大互动娱乐创始人
